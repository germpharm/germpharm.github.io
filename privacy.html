Privacy Policy

Effective: February 23, 2026

1. Our Approach

GermPharm LLC builds software that processes data locally by default. We believe privacy is an architecture, not a feature. We do not track you. We do not sell your data. We do not use analytics or cookies on this website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary for the services you use:

  • Account information: Name and email address when you create an account or contact us
  • Transaction data: Order details, shipping addresses, and payment information processed through our e-commerce platforms
  • Business communications: Emails and messages you send us directly

We do not collect browsing data, device fingerprints, location data, or any other tracking information.

3. How We Use Information

  • Fulfilling orders and providing customer support
  • Processing payments and managing accounts
  • Communicating about orders, services, or inquiries you have initiated
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not run analytics scripts
  • We do not collect health information through our website
  • We do not share or sell personal data to third parties for marketing

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is processed locally where possible. When cloud services are required, we use encrypted connections and store data with providers that meet industry security standards. We retain data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected.

6. Healthcare Data (HIPAA)

Any healthcare-related data processed through our professional services is handled in compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Patient health information is never stored on, transmitted through, or accessible from our public-facing services.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services for business operations:

  • Shopify: E-commerce order processing
  • QuickBooks: Accounting and invoicing
  • UPS: Shipping and logistics

Each service has its own privacy policy. We share only the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
