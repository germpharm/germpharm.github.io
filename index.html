What We're Building

The current AI model trains on the internet, serves answers from a black box, and credits nobody. Knowledge extracted, anonymized, commoditized.

We're building the opposite.

The AI becomes the routing layer, not the knowledge layer. It figures out which human experience is most relevant to your problem and connects you to it. The model doesn't replace the humans. It makes the humans findable.

Every node is a real person solving real problems. The memory files are structured experience, not scraped text. Attribution is structural, not optional. Privacy is the architecture, not a feature. Everything stays local unless you choose to share.

The model is the scaffolding. The memory is the product. The network is the moat.
